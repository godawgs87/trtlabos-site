Privacy Policy

How TRT LabOS collects, uses, and protects your personal and health information.

Last updated: May 1, 2026 · Effective: May 1, 2026 · Questions? support@trtlabos.com

HIPAA notice: TRT LabOS handles health information in a manner consistent with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Your lab results and health data are treated as Protected Health Information (PHI) and are never sold, rented, or shared for advertising purposes.

1. Who we are

TRT LabOS, LLC ("TRT LabOS," "we," "us," or "our") operates trtlabos.com and provides at-home lab testing, AI-powered health insights, and clinic-connected reporting services for individuals on testosterone replacement therapy (TRT). We are based in Indianapolis, Indiana.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of our services:

  • Account information: Name, email address, date of birth, password.
  • Health information: Lab results, biomarker values, protocol details (medication, dosage, frequency), and any health context you choose to share.
  • Shipping information: Mailing address and phone number used to send your Tasso collection device.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely through our payment provider. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and device/browser information collected via standard web analytics.
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team or through the contact form.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your lab test orders.
  • Deliver your results and AI-generated insights to your dashboard.
  • Share your results with your connected TRT clinic, only when you have authorized this sharing.
  • Send transactional communications (order confirmations, result notifications, reminders).
  • Improve the accuracy and relevance of our AI interpretation models.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not use your health information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with employers or insurance companies.

4. Clinic data sharing

When you connect a TRT clinic to your account, you authorize TRT LabOS to share your lab results and AI-generated reports with that clinic before your scheduled appointments. You may revoke clinic access at any time from your account settings. Revoking access stops future sharing but does not delete records already delivered to the clinic.

5. Laboratory partner

Your biological samples are processed by a CLIA-certified laboratory partner. We share your name, date of birth, and test order with this partner for the sole purpose of processing your sample and returning results. Our laboratory partner is contractually prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

6. Data security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access to health data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • We conduct regular security assessments of our systems and third-party integrations.
  • In the event of a data breach affecting your health information, we will notify you in compliance with applicable law.

7. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain session state and understand how visitors use the site. We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not build individual user profiles or share data with advertising networks. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly as a result.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal and health information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account.
  • Delete your account and associated data, subject to any legal retention requirements.
  • Restrict clinic sharing at any time.
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@trtlabos.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data retention

We retain your account information and health records for as long as your account is active and for a period thereafter as required by applicable law or medical record regulations. When you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

10. Children's privacy

TRT LabOS services are not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at support@trtlabos.com.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or by displaying a notice on your dashboard before the changes take effect. Continued use of TRT LabOS after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
