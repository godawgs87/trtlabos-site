Why clinics connect
Everything you need. Zero added workflow.

Zero manual steps

Results are auto-delivered to your clinic the moment they're ready — no faxes, no patient emails, no login-sharing. The PDF is formatted and in your hands before the appointment starts.

Flags before you open the chart

Elevated hematocrit, E2 imbalance, declining HDL — TRT LabOS flags abnormal markers and surfaces them in your portal before the appointment. You walk in knowing what to discuss.

Patient compliance improves

Testing at a lab requires scheduling, driving, and waiting — so patients skip it. At-home testing with a Tasso device removes every barrier. Your patients show up tested. Every appointment.
